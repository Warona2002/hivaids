Understanding HIV Medication Adherence

Adhering to HIV medication is essential for living a healthy life. Take your medication consistently to reduce viral load, prevent resistance, and improve your well-being.

Why Adherence is Important?

  • Helps reduce transmission risks.
  • Prevents drug resistance.
  • Boosts the immune system.
