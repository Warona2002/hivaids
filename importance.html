The Importance of Medication Adherence

Staying on your medication can save your life. Find out why adherence matters!

Why Adherence is Crucial

Adhering to your HIV treatment is the key to managing the virus and preventing complications. Without proper adherence, you risk the virus developing resistance to medication, leading to ineffective treatment and the possibility of drug resistance.

"Taking your medication as prescribed helps you live a longer, healthier life and reduces the risk of transmitting HIV."
- Health Expert

Benefits of Medication Adherence

Reduced Risk of Complications

Adhering to ART helps lower the chances of opportunistic infections and other health complications.

Improved Quality of Life

Consistent medication helps people with HIV live healthier lives and prevent unnecessary hospital visits.

Prevention of Transmission

Taking your medication reduces the viral load, making it less likely to transmit the virus to others.
