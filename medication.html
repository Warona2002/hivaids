HIV Medication and Treatment

HIV treatment involves antiretroviral therapy (ART), which helps individuals with HIV live long and healthy lives. Adhering to medication is crucial for managing the virus effectively.

Types of HIV Medication

  • Entry inhibitors – Prevent the virus from entering cells.
  • Reverse transcriptase inhibitors – Block the virus from making copies of itself.
  • Protease inhibitors – Prevent the virus from maturing.
  • Integrase inhibitors – Stop the virus from integrating into human DNA.

How to Take HIV Medication Effectively

  • Take medication at the same time daily.
  • Do not skip doses to prevent drug resistance.
  • Follow your doctor's instructions carefully.
  • Maintain regular check-ups for monitoring.
